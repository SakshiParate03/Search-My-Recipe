Burger

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound (450g) ground beef (preferably 80/20 blend)
  2. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  5. 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. 4 hamburger buns
  7. Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, cheese slices, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.

Instructions:

Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat. In a large bowl, gently mix together the ground beef, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder until just combined.
Be careful not to overmix, as this can make the burgers tough.
Divide the meat mixture into 4 equal portions, then shape each portion into a patty about 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick.
Make an indentation in the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent it from puffing up while cooking.
Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or longer if you prefer your burgers more well-done.
Make sure to only flip the burgers once to ensure proper cooking.
While the burgers are cooking, you can toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a separate skillet until lightly golden brown.
Once the burgers are cooked to your desired doneness, remove them from the grill or skillet and let them rest for a few minutes.
Assemble your burgers by placing each patty on a toasted bun and adding your favorite toppings.
Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade burgers!
Feel free to customize this recipe by adding different seasonings to the ground beef mixture or experimenting with various toppings to suit your taste preferences.
